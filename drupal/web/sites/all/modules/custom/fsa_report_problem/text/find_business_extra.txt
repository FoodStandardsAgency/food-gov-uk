<h3>Overview</h3>

<p>You can use this service to report issues such as:</p>

<ul>
	<li>poor food hygiene</li>
	<li>a foreign object&nbsp;in your food</li>
	<li>cleanliness of facilities</li>
</ul>

<p>Once you submit your report it will be sent to the relevant local authority to investigate.</p>
